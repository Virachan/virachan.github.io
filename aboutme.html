About Me

Born and brought up in one of the busiest metropolitan cities of the world – Mumbai – I have always been surrounded by an interesting mix of people, plenty of activities, and a multicultural community. As a child, I loved going to my school – Pune Vidyarthi Griha’s Vidya Bhawan (India) – where I spent some of the best time of my life and completed a formative journey of 12 years. This was the time when my fascination for Astronomy took birth. I read several Astronomy and space science books, some of which I received as prizes from my participation in extra-curricular activities. In the first year of my Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Ramnarain Ruia College (India), I took up Computer Science as one of my subjects that introduced me to command-line interface and programming in C. Surprising or not, it shaped my life!

Moving to Canada with my family in 2011 was a mighty leap, not because it was a 20-hour long flight trip, but because it landed me on the track that I was destined to be on – the “astronomical” track! The four years of my Honours BSc degree in Astronomical Sciences at the University of Toronto Mississauga offered me the skill set that I needed to pursue my Master of Science (MSc) and Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degrees, both in Astronomy at The University of Western Ontario (Western). During the seven years of my graduate school and a year of postdoctoral research, I travelled great distances to 18 cities in 5 countries across 4 continents on the globe – a genuine adventure that perfectly blended recreation with my work!! Under the impeccable guidance of my mentors and supervisors, and through my professional training at world-class facilities and institutions, I moulded myself into a researcher, educator, and communicator.

Outside my work sphere, I am an art-loving person, always enthusiastic about creating something new! My free time is ‘me’ time. Dancing, singing, and free-hand designing are outlets of self-expression for me. At other times, I am a self-motivated writer. Through my graduate school travel, I discovered how much I love visiting places and making new connections with people. As an Astronomer, I believe reaching out to people and sharing stories makes it possible to connect the dots and bring the universe down to Earth!
