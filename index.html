A Special Session for the 2025 Users' Meeting Participants from our Waimea Control Room

As preparations are underway for the CFHT Users' Meeting in May 2025, we will host a special session for the participants from our Waimea Control Room on April 28, 2025 at 5 am HST. CFHT Behind the Scenes - Bienvenue! E komo mai! will feature a panel conversation with the CFHT staff and our Executive Director to show how CFHT staff members from different teams prepare in tamdem for a remote observing night and why is CFHT significant as a scientific facility on Maunakea. I am thrilled to chair the panel comprising of diverse expertise and look forward to welcoming the Users' Meeting participants ahead of the meeting!

Shadow the Scientists Program Takes Off at CFHT

2025 marks the year when CFHT held its first Shadow the Scientits (StS) event from our remote control room in Waimea for the public at the Maunakea Visitor Station (MKVIS). StS is an initiative to showcase science happening in real time to one and all, particularly the underpriveleged communities around the world. On February 26, 2025, I was thrilled to launch our first StS event with our Remote Observers, Jessica Nagasako and Alexis Acohido, and the StS Coordinator, Jameeka Marshall to show how we set up for a remote observing night. There was an impressive flux of people throughout the hour-and-a-half of the event with approximately 50 participants in total. Mahalo to the MKVIS and CFHT staff for their support in this endeavour!

Canadian Resident Astronomer at Canada-France-Hawai‘i Telescope

It has been just over a month since I joined the CFHT ‘Ohana as a Resident Astronomer, in the beautiful city of Kamuela (Waimea). In addition to my research on active galactic nuclei (AGN; supermassive black holes millions to several billions of solar masses) and next-generation AGN time-domain surveys, I will be supporting night-time observations by scheduling obseving programs with different instruments on board CFHT as well as contributing to software development for scientific data processing and handling the instruments that take the data.
